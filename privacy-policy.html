Privacy Policy for milandgame

This Privacy Policy explains how milandgame ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects your information when you use our applications available on the Google Play Store, including information collected through Google AdMob advertising services.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: When you create an account or use our applications, we may collect personal information such as your name, email address, and other contact information.
  • Device Information: We may collect information about the device you use to access our applications, including the device model, operating system, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
  • Usage Information: We may collect information about how you use our applications, including the actions you take, the content you view, and the time and duration of your use.
  • Location Information: If you enable location services, we may collect precise or approximate location information from your device.
  • Advertising Data: We collect advertising identifiers (such as Android Advertising ID) and other data used for ad targeting and analytics purposes through the AdMob SDK.
  • Other Information: We may also collect other information that does not directly identify you but may be associated with your personal information, such as analytics data and usage statistics.

How We Collect Information

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Directly from You: We collect information when you voluntarily provide it to us, such as when you create an account, contact us for support, or participate in surveys.
  • Automatically: We collect information automatically when you use our applications, including through the use of cookies, web beacons, and other tracking technologies.
  • Through AdMob SDK: Google AdMob SDK automatically collects certain device and usage data to provide advertising services, including ad targeting, ad delivery, and performance measurement.
  • From Third Parties: We may collect information from third-party sources, such as advertising networks and analytics providers, in accordance with their privacy policies.

How We Use Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our applications and services.
  • To understand how users interact with our applications.
  • To develop new features, products, and services.
  • To personalize your experience and provide content and recommendations tailored to your interests.
  • To communicate with you about your account, updates, and other relevant information.
  • To send you marketing and promotional materials, with your consent.
  • To display targeted advertisements through AdMob that may be relevant to your interests.
  • To measure ad performance and optimize advertising campaigns.
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues, security breaches, and fraud.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms of service.

Sharing of Information

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: We may share your information with third parties when you have given us your explicit consent to do so.
  • With Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf, such as hosting, analytics, and payment processing.
  • With Google AdMob: We share device and usage data with Google AdMob to enable ad delivery, targeting, and analytics services. Google may use this data in accordance with its own privacy policies.
  • With Advertising Partners: We may share aggregated or anonymized data with advertising partners to improve ad relevance and performance.
  • For Legal Reasons: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.
  • In Connection with a Business Transaction: If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.
  • Aggregated or Anonymous Information: We may share aggregated or anonymous information that does not identify you personally for various purposes, such as analytics and market research.

Google AdMob SDK Data Practices

Our applications integrate Google AdMob SDK to display advertisements and generate revenue. The AdMob SDK collects and processes certain data as described below:

1. Data Collected by AdMob SDK

  • Android Advertising ID (AAID) or other advertising identifiers
  • Device model, manufacturer, and operating system version
  • IP address and network information
  • App usage data (session duration, interactions with ads)
  • Approximate location (based on IP address or device settings, if permitted)
  • Language and time zone settings
  • Screen resolution and device orientation
  • Network type (Wi-Fi, cellular, etc.)

2. Purpose of Data Collection by AdMob

  • To deliver relevant advertisements to users
  • To measure ad impressions and clicks
  • To prevent ad fraud and ensure ad delivery quality
  • To optimize ad performance and user experience
  • To provide analytics on ad performance to us
  • To comply with Google's advertising policies and legal requirements

3. Data Sharing by AdMob

  • Google may share collected data with its advertising partners to enable targeted advertising
  • Data may be used by Google to improve its advertising services across its platforms
  • Personal data is not shared with advertisers unless explicitly authorized by the user
  • Aggregated and anonymized data may be shared with third parties for market research

4. User Controls for AdMob Data Collection

  • Users can reset their Android Advertising ID at any time in device settings
  • Users can opt out of personalized advertising in Google Settings > Ads
  • Users can disable ad tracking in their device settings
  • Opting out of personalized ads will still show ads, but they will not be based on user interests

Google's privacy policy for AdMob can be found at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

For more information about Google's advertising practices and how to opt out of personalized ads, visit: https://adssettings.google.com

Security Measures

We take the security of your information seriously and implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect it from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Encryption of sensitive data transmitted over the internet.
  • Use of secure servers and networks to store and process your information.
  • Regular security assessments and updates to our systems.
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms to limit access to your information to authorized personnel.
  • Training of our employees on data security and privacy best practices.
  • Compliance with Google's data security requirements for AdMob integration.
  • Implementation of industry-standard security protocols for third-party SDK integrations.

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee absolute security. Data collected by AdMob is subject to Google's security measures as outlined in Google's privacy policy.

Your Choices

You have the following choices regarding your information:

  • Access and Update: You can access and update your personal information by logging into your account or contacting us.
  • Delete Your Account: You can request to delete your account and personal information at any time.
  • Opt-Out of Marketing: You can opt out of receiving marketing communications from us by following the instructions in those communications or contacting us.
  • Disable Location Services: You can disable location services on your device if you do not want us to collect location information.
  • Manage Cookies: You can manage cookies and other tracking technologies through your browser settings.
  • Control Ad Tracking: You can reset your Android Advertising ID or opt out of personalized ads in your device's Google settings.
  • Limit AdMob Data Collection: You can adjust ad settings in Google Ads Settings to control how your data is used for advertising purposes.

To manage your Google ad settings, visit: https://myaccount.google.com/ads-settings
